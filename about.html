The Freshy Cheffy Story

Established 2021

Born in the LBC

What started as a small neighborhood initiative in Long Beach has blossomed into a movement. We noticed that nearly 40% of perfectly good produce never leaves the farm because of minor aesthetic imperfections. Our founder, a local chef, saw an opportunity to connect these "ugly" gems with families looking to eat healthy without the high price tag.

Why It
Matters.

The current food system is broken. We're here to fix it, one box at a time.

The Price of Perfection

Every year, billions of pounds of food are wasted because they are the "wrong" shape, size, or color. This artificial standard drives up food costs for everyone while farmers are forced to discard their hard work.

Rising Food Insecurity

With inflation at record highs, fresh produce has become a luxury for many. By rescuing food, we can offer premium organics at prices that make sense for everyday families.

Climate Impact

Food waste in landfills produces methane, a greenhouse gas far more potent than CO2. If food waste were a country, it would be the third-largest emitter in the world.

Our Coastal
Commitment

Based out of Long Beach, California, our operations are inspired by the ocean. We use plastic-free packaging, ocean-cooled transport, and partner with local coastal farms to ensure the shortest trip from soil to table.
