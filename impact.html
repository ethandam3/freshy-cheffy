Why It
Matters.

The current food system is broken. We're here to fix it, one box at a time.

The Price of Perfection

Every year, billions of pounds of food are wasted because they are the "wrong" shape, size, or color. This artificial standard drives up food costs for everyone while farmers are forced to discard their hard work.

Rising Food Insecurity

With inflation at record highs, fresh produce has become a luxury for many. By rescuing food, we can offer premium organics at prices that make sense for everyday families.

Climate Impact

Food waste in landfills produces methane, a greenhouse gas far more potent than CO2. If food waste were a country, it would be the third-largest emitter in the world.
